## Visual Treatment

Use three visual modes:

1. Polished concept images for big ideas.
2. Actual SSRM screenshots for repo credibility.
3. Clean text/diagram cards for definitions and falsifiers.

Do not overuse text. The video should feel like a story, not a slide deck.

## Suggested B-Roll To Record From Repo

If recording live visuals from the repo, capture:

- SSRM-3D desktop visualization;
- pressure-layer visualization page;
- learned-integration visualization;
- quick scroll through `docs/public/README.md`;
- quick scroll through `docs/12_evidence_matrix.md`;
- manifest snippet showing `72` runs and `0` failures.

## Sound Design

- Sensor pulse for perception sections.
- Low heartbeat-like pulse for threat/affect sections.
- Soft glitch or data drop for interruption/continuity.
- Clean click or switch for ablation moments.
- Music drop for no-leak negative result.

## Editing Warnings

- Do not imply the agents are conscious.
- Do not imply the screenshots are biologically realistic.
- Do not hide the partial negative result.
- Do not frame the toy scale as a weakness to apologize for; frame it as controlled evidence.
- Do not overuse "emergence" without explaining the boundary.
